Vehicle Purchase Assistance

Our vehicle purchase assistance program, a program designed to assist people with an automobile purchase. Buying a vehicle is a difficult process at best. We take the anxiety out of the purchase. We can assist in any or all of the purchasing steps:

  • Analyze which vehicle(s), new or used, will best fit your needs. We objectively analyze your individual needs and come up with a list of vehicle models that will meet those needs. We will provide you with the pros and cons of each vehicle on the list.

  • Determine what equipment you require/desire on a vehicle. Most vehicles can be equipped many different ways. Proper Equipment on a vehicle makes a huge difference in the ability of that vehicle to meet your needs.   • Maximize your rebates and incentives. We will make sure you maximize the rebates and incentives that you receive.

  • Negotiate the best price. We use our extensive knowledge of vehicle pricing and negotiating strategies to negotiate the best deal possible.. We will negotiate any vehicle anywhere in the United States.

  • Selling your old vehicle. The sale or trade-in of your old vehicle can make a huge economic difference on your transaction. We will assist you with all the alternatives to help you get the best value for your old vehicle. We may even be able to work out a deal in which you sell your vehicle yourself and get a sales tax credit for the sales price of the used vehicle off of the cost of the replacement vehicle (this option is available in most states).

  • Select the best way to pay for your vehicle. We will help you analyze the various options for payment and determine which way is best for you. We cannot get a vehicle loan for you that you do not qualify for.

These steps will vary based on your individual needs. Pricing for these services will be determined on an individual basis. Generally, we charge by the hour so we can give you as much or as little assistance as you need.
